This coming Saturday will be the first day of our 3-week intensive Mysore practice. Jamsai and I are looking forward to helping you find the right technique for your body and improve your skills for challenging movements and asanas.

For most of you, you have experienced practicing with us before, so we expect you to be a good example to your classmates of how to properly behave during Mysore practice.

Traditional Mysore Culture

💧 No water inside the Mysore room
📵 No phone or camera inside the Mysore room
🤫 No talking during practice
🧘 Do not move your mat around (unless teacher requests)
🤝 Do not help other students during practice (unless teacher requests)
🔄 Follow proper vinyasa — no stopping to look around or watch others
🙏 Before finishing (after backbending), wait in front of your mat with arms crossed to chest to wait for the teacher to assist your last backbending

We love to see you get through all the obstacles in practice and look forward to working on your technique.

— Boonchu Tanti & Jamsai Tanti
