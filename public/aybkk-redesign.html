01
✦ About

A legacy
of practice.

At AYBKK, we follow the full spectrum of Ashtanga — gaze, breath, posture, bandha. Not a fitness class. A method practiced exactly as it has been for generations, under direct lineage from Mysore, India.

We offer Mysore and Led Primary classes on the traditional schedule. We have been running a Led Intermediate Series for nearly a decade — a rarity that reflects the depth of our students.

04
✦ Your Teacher
Boonchu Tanti
Lead Teacher · AYBKK

Honest
teaching.

"We find your weaknesses. We match teaching to your energy. That is how you grow — not faster, but correctly."

Boonchu Tanti has practiced and taught Ashtanga for over ten years, studying under the lineage of Pattabhi Jois and Sharath Jois in Mysore, India. His teaching is direct, personal, and built around each individual — not a generic class.

Together with Jamsai Tanti, AYBKK has built a community of practitioners committed to the full method: six days a week, every week, without compromise.
